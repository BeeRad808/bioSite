About Me

Matthew McKnight III was born on July 19th, 2003 on the island of Oahu. Matthew is a substitute teacher and avid plant collector. Matthew is a member of multiple orchid societies on Oahu, and an American Orchid Society member. He is also a board member of the Ewa Orchid Society, a group made up of orchid enthusiasts from around the world with a base in Ewa, Hawaii.

Growing up on a green onion farm, Matthew was constantly surrounded by plants. His great grandmother was an influential figure towards his passion for plants. He recalls being fascinated by how well she grew Dendrobiums in her backyard.
